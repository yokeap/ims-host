TOWING TANK TEST

The towing tank test set is used for ship design and to determine various parameters related to ship design.

Distinctive features
Dimension
- Length 80 m, Width 4 m, Depth 3.5 m
Wave maker
- Generating regular
- Waves 8 segments wave maker max.
- Wave height : 0.5 m.
- Wave period : 0.5-4 s.
Wave absorber
- Wave absorption rate of no less than 90%
An electric overhead crane
- Distance 15 m
- Capable of lifting loads 5 tons
- Remote control system with a length of at least 30 m
Carriage Speed: 0-4 m/s

WAVE BASIN

The wave basin is used for coastal studies.

Dimension
- Length 20 m, Width 9 m, Depth 1.5 m
Wave Generator
- Adjust wave height is 0.00 m. to 0.50 m.
- Adjust the wave height level is 5 level.
- The constant height of no less than 10 min.
- The length of the wave probe sensor wire is 200 cm.
